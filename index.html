Enriched Pharmecy

Enriched Pharmacy is a concept that focuses on providing comprehensive pharmaceutical services beyond just dispensing medications. It aims to enhance patient care by offering personalized health consultations, medication management, and wellness programs. Enriched pharmacies often provide additional services such as health screenings, immunizations, and chronic disease management, ensuring a holistic approach to healthcare.

Medical Treatment

Medical treatment encompasses a wide range of services aimed at diagnosing, managing, and curing illnesses or injuries. It involves various healthcare professionals, including doctors, nurses, and specialists, who work together to provide comprehensive care. Medical treatment can include medication, surgery, therapy, and other interventions tailored to the patient's specific needs. The goal is to improve the patient's health and quality of life through effective and timely medical interventions.
